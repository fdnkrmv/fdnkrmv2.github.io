Explainable AI is essential when machine learning systems inform decisions in high-stakes environments. I am interested in methods that help users understand which signals influence model behaviour and how predictions change across spatial, temporal, and contextual conditions.

My goal is to support AI systems that are not only accurate, but also inspectable, accountable, and communicable to technical and non-technical audiences.
