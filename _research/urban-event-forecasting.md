Urban event forecasting focuses on anticipating when and where important events may occur in cities. My work in this area includes crime prediction and broader context-aware modelling for dynamic urban systems.

I focus on approaches that combine predictive performance with operational usefulness: models should account for local context, temporal change, data quality, and the constraints faced by decision-makers.
