![Remote sensing](/images/research-remote-sensing.svg)

Remote sensing provides a valuable view of urban and environmental change. I am interested in how satellite, aerial, and other geospatial data sources can be integrated with administrative and event data to improve situational awareness.

This area supports richer spatial context for prediction tasks, especially where built environment, land use, mobility, and environmental signals influence urban outcomes.
