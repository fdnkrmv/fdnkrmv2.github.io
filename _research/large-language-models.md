Large language models offer new ways to retrieve, summarise, and reason over complex information. My interest is in using retrieval-augmented generation and knowledge augmentation to support context-aware analysis rather than treating language models as standalone black boxes.

This includes connecting structured data, domain knowledge, and textual evidence so generated outputs are more grounded, transparent, and useful for real-world analytical workflows.
