[Course profile](https://programs-courses.uq.edu.au/course.html?course_code=INFS7901)

Data Science and Analytics develops practical and conceptual skills for using data science methods to analyse real-world problems and communicate evidence-based insights.

In this course, I mentored students through open-ended analytics projects, helping them move from problem framing and data preparation to modelling, interpretation, and communication.

Teaching responsibilities included:

* Mentored students through complex data projects from initial concept to working solution.
* Supported applied analytics teaching across laboratory-based and project-based environments.
* Built capability to extend teaching into GIS, spatial data science, GeoAI, and field-based geospatial learning environments.
