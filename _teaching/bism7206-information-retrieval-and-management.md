Information Retrieval and Management covers methods for organising, retrieving, evaluating, and managing information in modern data-rich environments.

In this course, I supported postgraduate students in understanding retrieval concepts, search and ranking methods, evaluation practices, and the practical implications of information access systems.

Teaching responsibilities included:

* Delivered applied tutorials in laboratory and analytics settings.
* Collaborated with course coordinators on assessment and learning material improvement.
* Supported students in translating retrieval and information management concepts into practical project outcomes.
