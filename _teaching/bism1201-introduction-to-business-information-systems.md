Introduction to Business Information Systems introduces students to the role of information systems in organisations, including how data, processes, systems, and digital technologies support business decision-making and operations.

In this course, I delivered tutorials and supported students in connecting foundational information systems concepts with practical analytical and organisational problems.

Teaching responsibilities included:

* Delivered tutorials across undergraduate and postgraduate courses to 500+ students, translating abstract computational and analytical concepts into practical, applicable skills.
* Collaborated with course coordinators on curriculum improvement, including assessment design and learning materials aligned with emerging research and industry practice.
* Mentored students through complex data and systems projects from initial concept to working solution.
