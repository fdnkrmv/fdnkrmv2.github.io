Data Management and Business Intelligence focuses on how organisations collect, manage, query, transform, and analyse data to support evidence-based decisions.

In this course, I helped students build practical skills in data modelling, database concepts, business intelligence workflows, and communicating analytical results.

Teaching responsibilities included:

* Delivered tutorials across undergraduate and postgraduate courses to 500+ students, translating abstract computational and analytical concepts into practical, applicable skills.
* Supported students in developing structured problem-solving and communication skills for data-driven projects.
* Contributed to applied analytics learning activities that connected technical methods with organisational decision-making.
