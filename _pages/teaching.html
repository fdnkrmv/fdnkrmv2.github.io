As an academic instructor at The University of Queensland, I am responsible for delivering tutorials across diverse and large undergraduate and postgraduate cohorts, including both laboratory-based and classroom-based teaching. To date, I have taught more than 500 students, translating complex computational and analytical concepts into practical, applicable skills.

My broader teaching responsibilities include:

  • Designing and delivering tutorials in laboratory and classroom settings across multiple courses.
  • Collaborating with course coordinators on assessment design, curriculum improvement, and learning materials aligned with current research and industry practice.
  • Mentoring students through open-ended, data-driven projects from problem framing to final solution and communication.
  • Supporting capability to extend teaching into GIS, spatial data science, GeoAI, and field-based geospatial learning environments.
